Interests

Throughout my life, I have always been very interested in science. I was fascinated by all the amazing animals that lived on Earth, and by the fact that we exist in an enormous galaxy in an unimaginablely large space. I was always finding ways to absorb more knowledge about biology and astronomy, as those were the most interesting fields of science for me. As a small child in elementary school, I remember that I would love going to the library to borrow small animal books and documentaries on DVD's. Today, even though I don't search for interesting information as often, I am still very much interested in those subjects. Another one of my interests is swimming. Being on the swim team has been an amazing experience. I've met so many great friends on the team, and it provides me with many opportunities to compete, and show off team pride.

My Hobbies

My favorite things to do are to hang out with my friends and swim. I can enjoy just about anything if I do it with my good friends. From going to each other's houses to going camping to competing at swim meets, it's always a fun time for all of us if we're together. I am always looking for opportunities to hang out with my friends, which is particularly difficult to do because my friends are all spread out across multiple high schools. My favorite sport and past time is swimming because it is a great combination of exercise and competitiveness. When I have time at home, I enjoy watching funny videos on YouTube, occasionally watching tv, and playing Black Ops 2 on my PS3 with friends.
